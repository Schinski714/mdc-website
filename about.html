About

Built on Relationships, Not Transactions.

MDC Home Services started in Malvern, Pennsylvania, with a simple idea: build a company that higher-end homeowners would want to keep calling. No one wants to find a new contractor every year. We wanted to be the last one you had to find.

Why We Exist

The home services industry is broken in a very specific way. There's a race to the bottom on price, and a race to the bottom on quality, and homeowners end up bouncing between contractors who ghost them, overcharge them, or can't actually do the work they promised.

We built MDC for the people who are tired of that. Clients who own homes worth taking care of. Clients who would rather pay a little more for a team that shows up, communicates, and leaves the job right. Clients who want a relationship, not a transaction.

That's the whole thing. We're not trying to be the biggest. We're trying to be the one you refer your friends to without hesitation.

Two Markets, One Team

We started on the Main Line and serve Malvern, Wayne, Devon, Paoli, Villanova, Bryn Mawr, Gladwyne, Radnor, Berwyn, and the surrounding towns. Over time, our clients started asking us to handle their Shore houses too. So we started traveling.

Today we serve Ocean City, Sea Isle City, Avalon, and Stone Harbor with the same team, same standards, and same relationship-first approach. For our dual-home clients, it's one call, one team, one set of keys they trust. That's an unusual thing in this industry, and it's the thing our clients value most.

What We Believe

Premium Doesn't Mean Expensive. It Means Right.

We use materials that last. We use hardware that doesn't fail. We do the work correctly the first time. That costs more upfront and less over ten years.

Communication Is Craft.

The number one complaint about contractors isn't workmanship. It's communication. We treat daily updates, clear timelines, and honest answers as part of the job.

If We Can't Do It Right, We Won't Do It.

We'd rather turn down a job than cut corners to win it. If we're not the right team for what you need, we'll tell you and recommend someone who is.

Relationships Compound.

The best business development we do is doing good work for the clients we already have. Almost all of our new business comes from referrals and repeat clients. We plan to keep it that way.

Want to Work With Us?

Start with a walkthrough. It's how every one of our long-term client relationships has started.

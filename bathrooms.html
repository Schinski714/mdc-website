Services

Bathrooms

Primary suites, powder rooms, and guest baths built to feel like the best hotel you've stayed in. Walk-in showers with zero-entry tile, heated floors, quiet exhaust, real stone. Done properly, under budget, on time.

The Bathroom Is the Room Where Shortcuts Show

A bad tile job reveals itself in six months. A cheap vanity looks cheap the day it's installed. A drain that wasn't sloped properly becomes your problem for the next ten years. Bathrooms are where the difference between a premium contractor and a discount one becomes obvious.

We do bathrooms the way we'd do them for ourselves. Waterproofing done right before tile goes down. Real stone, real tile, real fixtures, not the builder-grade stuff marked up 400%. Ventilation that actually works. Lighting you can read by and relax in.

On the Shore, we use materials that handle humidity and salt. On the Main Line, we match the feel of the rest of the house. Every bathroom is different, and none of them get templated.

What We Don't Cut Corners On

Waterproofing

Schluter-KERDI or equivalent membrane behind every tile. This is the one place contractors cut cost and it's the one place you pay for it later.

Real Materials

Stone, porcelain, solid wood, quality fixtures. We won't install anything we wouldn't put in our own home.

Ventilation

Properly sized quiet exhaust, vented outside. Not into the attic. Mold and moisture damage come from ignoring this.

Level, Plumb, Square

Tile lines that actually line up. Grout that's consistent. Edges that meet. The details you stop noticing because nothing's wrong.

Plumbing Done Right

Proper slopes, proper rough-in, shutoffs where they should be. If we have to open a wall five years later, we can.

The Finish

Caulk lines clean. Silicone where silicone belongs. Hardware installed so it doesn't wobble. The stuff you feel every morning.

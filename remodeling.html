Services

Remodeling & Renovation

Kitchens, whole-home renovations, additions, and the kind of work that changes how a house lives. We take on fewer projects than most contractors on purpose, and we stay on them until they're done right.

What We Take On

Full kitchen renovations. Primary suite additions. Whole-home renovations. Interior reconfigurations that open up a dated floor plan. Historic home updates that respect the bones. Shore house gut renovations that have to survive salt, sand, and renters.

We're not the cheapest quote you'll get. We're not trying to be. What we offer is a team that treats your home like it's ours, communicates daily, and finishes what we start when we said we would.

Most of our remodeling clients come from referrals. Most of them end up hiring us again for the next project, or for the Shore house a year later. That's the game, do the work well enough that you never have to find another contractor.

Typical Timeline

6 to 20 weeks depending on scope. Full gut renovations run longer. You'll know the timeline before we start.

How a Remodel With Us Actually Goes

The short version: you know what's happening, when, and why. Always.

01

Walkthrough & Scope

We come to your house, look at the space, and talk through what you actually want. We ask a lot of questions. We make suggestions when you want them and stay quiet when you don't.

02

Design & Proposal

Detailed scope, line-item pricing, material specifications, timeline. You see everything. No surprises on invoice day.

03

Build

Clean jobsite. Daily progress notes. One project manager who's your contact. Subs we've worked with for years, not strangers we found last week.

04

Walkthrough & Warranty

You walk the finished space with us. If it's not right, we fix it before we leave. Then we stay available, most clients keep working with us long after the project ends.

Thinking About a Renovation?

Start with a walkthrough. No pressure, no hard sell. If we're not the right fit we'll tell you.

Decks & Outdoor Living

Custom decks, pergolas, and outdoor living spaces built to survive Pennsylvania winters and Shore summers. Ipe, mahogany, composite, cedar. Hidden fasteners, real railings, real framing underneath.

Most decks fail from the inside out

A deck is a structure. The boards on top get all the attention, but the joists, the ledger board, the flashing, and the footings are what keep people from getting hurt. Bad framing is invisible until it isn't, and by then, it's dangerous.

We build decks like we build additions. Properly engineered structure. Correct ledger board attachment with flashing. Galvanized or stainless hardware. Footings that go below the frost line. Then we build the finish on top of it, hidden fasteners, clean transitions, proper drainage between boards.

On the Shore, we use materials and hardware that can take the salt air. On the Main Line, we match the architecture of the house. A deck should look like it was always supposed to be there.

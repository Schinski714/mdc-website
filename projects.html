The Work

The Work We Take On

Our scope is intentional, not a menu. We do the work our team can do start to finish, to the standard our members expect. For everything else, we send you to people we trust.

01 · Day-to-Day

The List That Keeps a House Running

This is what our team does every week for our Home Care Plan members. Repairs, punch lists, finish work, the small things that keep a house sharp. It's unglamorous and it's the foundation of the relationship. A house that's kept up never needs a rescue.

Members get priority scheduling and member pricing. Non-members can book us for day or half-day blocks, subject to our schedule, and we'll tell you straight if we can fit you in.

We're not the cheapest crew in the zip code. We're the one your neighbors actually recommend.

What We Handle
  • • Drywall repair & paint touch-up
  • • Trim, baseboard, crown molding
  • • Door & hardware replacement
  • • Shelving & storage installs
  • • TV mounting & cable management
  • • Fixture swaps (lighting, faucets)
  • • Tile & grout repair
  • • Deck board & railing repairs
  • • Exterior caulking & sealant
  • • Minor carpentry & finish work
  • • Gutter cleaning & repair
  • • Pressure washing
02 · Select Larger Projects

A Small Number of Larger Projects Each Year. Members Only.

We take on three to five larger projects per year, exclusively for Home Care Plan members. That's it. We don't run a remodeling firm. We run a relationship with a short list of homeowners who want things done right, and a few times a year, one of those homeowners wants something bigger.

The constraint is intentional. It's how we guarantee the same team, same standard, same attention to detail whether we're tightening a handrail or rebuilding a primary bath. It's also why the waitlist exists.

Typical Scope
  • • Primary bathroom refreshes and rebuilds
  • • Finished basements, top to bottom
  • • Deck rebuilds and outdoor living spaces
  • • Kitchen refreshes (cabinets, counters, finishes)
  • • Screened porches and three-season rooms
  • • Historic home restoration and trim work
  • • Shore house refreshes between seasons
How It Works
  • • Member-only. Join the plan first.
  • • Booked 6 to 12 months in advance
  • • Detailed scope, line-item pricing, no surprises
  • • One project manager, daily updates
  • • Subs we've worked with for years
  • • Clean jobsite, always
  • • Walkthrough and warranty on completion

03 · Everything Else

For Bigger Work, We Send You to People We Trust

Gut renovations. Additions. Full kitchens with new footprints. Architectural work. New construction. That's not what we do, and we're not going to fake it to capture a job.

What we do is send you to a short list of builders, designers, and architects we've worked with for years. People who show up, communicate, and finish what they start. Home Care Plan members get warm introductions, and we stay involved as your advocate through the project, because a bad GC can wreck a house we're responsible for maintaining.

We don't take finder's fees on partner referrals. We don't need them, and the moment money flows that way, the recommendation isn't honest anymore.

Partner Work Includes
  • • Whole-home gut renovations
  • • Primary suite additions
  • • Second-story additions
  • • Full kitchen redesigns with new footprint
  • • Architectural and structural work
  • • New construction
  • • Large-scale historic restoration
Member Benefit

Home Care Plan members get warm introductions to our partners and our ongoing advocacy throughout the project.
